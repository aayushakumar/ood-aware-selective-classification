Key Takeaways

What we learned from this research

1

Training Data Matters

Models learn biases from their training data. If toxic comments in the dataset often mention certain groups, the model wrongly associates those groups with toxicity.

2

Cross-Domain Testing Reveals Weaknesses

A model that works great on Wikipedia might fail on Twitter. Always test on data from different sources than what you trained on.

3

Fairness and Accuracy Can Coexist

Our research shows that training on diverse data can actually IMPROVE both fairness AND accuracy‚Äîit's not always a trade-off!

4

Measure What Matters

Overall accuracy isn't enough. We must measure fairness across different identity groups to ensure AI doesn't discriminate.
